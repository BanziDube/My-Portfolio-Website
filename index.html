About Me

I'm a young and avid explorer of the tech space, guided by an unquenchable curiosity and a deep fascination for how software can solve real-world challenges. My journey began at the age of 12, during a school holiday, when I was invited into the cockpit of an airplane. Surrounded by an ocean of buttons and blinking lights, I found myself unable to stop smiling. A thought struck me: "If software can fly and land something as powerful as an airplane, what other extraordinary possibilities are waiting to be unlocked by software?"


That single moment sparked a lifelong quest to uncover the hidden potentials of technology. With every new challenge, I’m driven by the desire to innovate, adapt, and think creatively. I can say my mind is a sponge, always learning, always evolving. I see software not just as a tool, but as a universe of infinite ideas, and I’m excited to keep exploring every corner of it.
